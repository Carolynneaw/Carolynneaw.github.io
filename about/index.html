About Me

Hello! I am Carolynne Winkle and I am a senior graphic art major with a concentration in design. I also love to illustrate, so I’ve taken courses here at Taylor for that interest as well! After I graduate I hope to be able to use both my design and illustration skills for whatever it is I do next.

Personal

I love to draw for fun, but who’s surprised given that I already told you my major? Not me. I also love my dog back home, he is a mini golden doodle and his name is Henry. He is literally precious, although it’s possible I’m biased. If you want to see pictures of him I am more than happy to share.
